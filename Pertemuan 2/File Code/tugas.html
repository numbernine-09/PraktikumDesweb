M4 World Championship

Previous M3 World Tournament Winner 1. Blacklist Internasional 2. ONIC Philippines 3. BloodThirstyKings

The M4 World Championship is the final event and the World Championship of the 2022 Competitive Season organized by Moonton. Best teams throughout the year have qualified to this event.

The venue of M4 Group Stage changed from MPL Arena (XO Hall) to Bali United Studio and M4 Knockout Stage changed from Istora Senayan GBK to Tennis Indoor Stadium Senayan due to these unforeseen circumstances and in order to provide the best possible experience for fans.

About The Tournament

Format

  • Group Stage:
    January 1st - 4th, 2022
    • 16 teams are split into 4 groups of 4 teams.
    • Single round robin, all matches are played in a Bo1.
    • Group Top 2 advances to Playoffs Upper Bracket
    • Group Bottom 2 advances to Playoffs Lower Bracket
  • Knockout Stage:
    January 7st - 15th, 2022
    • Double elimination
    • Sixteen teams play in a double-elimination bracket.
      • Eight teams begin in the Upper Bracket, eight in the Lower Bracket.
      • First two rounds of Lower Bracket is Bo3, Grand Final is Bo7, all others matches are Bo5.
