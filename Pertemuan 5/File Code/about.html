Format

  • Regular Season: August 12th - October 2nd, 2022
    • Double Round Robin
    • All Matches are Bo3
      • Earn 1 point for each game win
      • Earn -1 point for each game lose
    • Top 6 teams will advance to playoffs
  • Playoffs: October 19th - October 23th, 2022
    • Hybrid Elimination
      • 3rd to 6th seeded teams compete in Round 1 (Single Elimination)
      • 1st and 2nd seeded teams recieved a round by and will start in Round 2 (Double Elimination)
    • All Matches except Grand Final is played in a Bo5
    • Grand Final is played in a Bo7
